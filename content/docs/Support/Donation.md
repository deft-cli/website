### If you want to support project, you can donate us:

https://www.patreon.com/c/xntas

## What you get

Honestly? Not much. And that's the point.

You get:
- My gratitude
- The feeling that you helped a kid build something real

That's it. No "early access", no "exclusive features", no "special status".

Everyone in the community is equal. Whether you pay $3 or $0, you're part of the same project.

## My promise

- No sponsorware (I won't hide code behind paywalls)
- No artificial limitations (free version is fully functional)
- No castes (everyone is equal)
- Transparent development (all roadmap is public on GitHub)

If you like what I'm doing — support me. If not — that's fine too.
The project is free.
